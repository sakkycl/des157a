Game Design Research

Febuary 27, 2024

In “Game Design UX Best Practices,” Amir Dori discusses the best the best practices and offers tips for designing games. The article gave me useful tips and practices that I can apply to my own project. For example, Dori explains that when designing mobile games, it is important to consider comfort-to-reach areas and hard-to-read areas because users are more likely to interact with elements in the comfort-to-reach areas. I believe this information is useful to consider when I make my website responsive. I can keep the comfort-to-reach areas in mind to ensure that the main interactive elements of my game, such as rolling the dice, is easily accessible to mobile players. This enhances usability and allows players to play my game with ease, gaining an overall better experience on my website.
