Visual Thinking Analysis

Febuary 15, 2024

The photo I chose shows an image of my future log spread for 2024. It is interesting because of the layout of the page, such as the mini pages within the page and icons for each mini calendar. This reflects how my bullet journal has evolved over the years. This image relates to the topic of my archive because my project is about some of my favorite bullet journal spreads that I have made. The collection says something about me because it documents parts of my life. For example, the future log shows any events or important dates in my life. The theme of the spread was also inspired by a character I adore, Miffy.

The photo shows an image of Gamcheon Culture Village. The most interest aspects of this image is the perspective, with the bright green car closest to the camera compared to the rest of the scenery. The natural sunlight and bright colors make the photo appear bustling with life. The most obvious aspect of the image is that it was taken in Korea. There is a high possibility that the project is a travel diary or a similar theme. The mysterious aspects of the image is the context, such as why the person at the village and took a photo of it. The photo makes me wonder if the person came to the location for a purpose or happened to stumblee upon it.
