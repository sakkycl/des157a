Research Form Design

January 25, 2025

The article, “Best practices for form design” by Salim Ansari discusses about the importance of good usability in form design in order to create a website or mobile application that converts. Ansari introduces a variety of practices to improve the usability of form design. For example, Ansari encourages using a one column layout because it does not disrupt vertical momentum of moving down the form. I believe all the Google forms I have ever encountered follow a one column layout. I found that the layout allows me to process the information more quickly and complete the form faster. Since we are learning how to use forms for our Studio 1 project, I think think using a one column layout will be helpful in increasing the usability of my own website. Overall, the article made me more aware of practices that I found useful when I needed to complete forms and taught me many ways to increase the usability of the forms I make in my future projects.
