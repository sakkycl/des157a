Visual Thinking Strategies Research

Febuary 8, 2024

In the article, “10 Intriguing Photographs to Teach Close Reading and Visual Thinking Skills,” Gonchar discusses the importance of practicing close reading and visual thinking skills consistently in order to help students become more visually literate and improve their overall academic skills. Gonchar offers a variety of images that can be used to practice these skills. As a visual learner and designer, I also think being visually literate is important. It allows me to utilize imagery to communicate in ways that words or other means cannot. For example, I found the Jessica Mille Architecte website utilizes images in a way that inspires visual thinking. When you first visit the page, there is an image that acts as a header for the website. However, when you scroll, the image shrinks and the logo is revealed. This animation utilizes a single image for two different purposes, demonstrating how versatile an image can be. In my own website, I would also like to be more mindful of imagery so I can better utilize them in a way that is exciting and engaging for viewers like the Jessica Mille Architecte website .
